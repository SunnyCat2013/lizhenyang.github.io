# haversine formula

球面上任意两点的中心角求解公式：  

$$
 hav(\frac{d}{r}) = hav(\phi_2 - \phi_1) + \cos(\phi_1)\cos(\phi_2)hav(\lambda_2 - \lambda_1) 
$$
